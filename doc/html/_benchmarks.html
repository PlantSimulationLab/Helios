Homogeneous Canopy Radiation Interception Benchmark

Description

This benchmark consists of a homogeneous canopy with LAI of 1.0 (5x5 cm2 leaves) and lateral extent of 20x20 m2 (laterally periodic boundary conditions) and height of 3 m. The canopy is irradiated by direct and diffuse sunlight.
